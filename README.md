# Kradin-Name-Generator
This repo helps you determine what your Kradin name would be.

### What is Kradin?

* **Kradin** is a name given to a child based on the **day of the week they are born**.
* It comes from the **Akan culture** (mainly in Ghana).
* The name connects the child to a **spiritual force or destiny** associated with that day.

---

### Day-of-Week Names (Kradin)

| Day       | Male Name | Female Name | Meaning / Note                  |
| --------- | --------- | ----------- | ------------------------------- |
| Sunday    | Kwasi     | Akosua      | Linked to leadership & vitality |
| Monday    | Kwadwo    | Adwoa       | Peaceful and calm nature        |
| Tuesday   | Kwabena   | Abena       | Energetic and adventurous       |
| Wednesday | Kwaku     | Akua        | Intelligent and adaptable       |
| Thursday  | Yaw       | Yaa         | Courageous and strong           |
| Friday    | Kofi      | Afia        | Friendly and wise               |
| Saturday  | Kwame     | Ama         | Balanced and spiritual          |

---

### Key Points

* Kradin is usually **the first name given at birth**.
* It reflects **spiritual identity and personal traits**.
* Other personal or family names are added later.

---

### What does kradin_name.py do?
kradin_name.py allows you to enter your d.o.b and sex to determine your Kradin name.

---

### What does today.py do?
For those that have just had a baby today, they can use today.py to determine what their babies Kradin name is.
